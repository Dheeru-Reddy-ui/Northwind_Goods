# Warranty

## Coverage
Electronics and mechanical products sold by Northwind Goods carry a **1-year
limited warranty** against manufacturing defects from the delivery date. This
covers faults in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## What's not covered
The warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, normal wear and tear, or
items modified by the customer. Consumable parts (for example, batteries) may
have shorter coverage.

## Making a warranty claim
Contact support with your order number and a description of the fault. If the
item is within warranty we'll arrange a repair, replacement, or refund. Warranty
claims outside the 30-day return window are handled as warranty service, not a
standard return.
