# Refund Process

## When you're eligible
A refund can be issued when your order is within the **30-day return window**,
has not already been refunded or cancelled, and the item is not a final-sale or
otherwise non-returnable product. Our support agent checks eligibility before
processing any refund.

## How refunds are issued
Eligible refunds go back to your **original payment method**. You'll receive an
email confirmation, and the amount typically appears within **5–7 business days**
depending on your bank.

## High-value refunds
For your protection, refunds above a set amount are reviewed by a human
specialist before they're released. If your refund needs review, the agent will
tell you it's being reviewed — you don't need to do anything else, and you'll get
a confirmation once it's approved.

## Partial refunds
In some cases (for example, a returned item arriving with missing parts) we may
issue a partial refund. The agent will explain the amount and the reason.
