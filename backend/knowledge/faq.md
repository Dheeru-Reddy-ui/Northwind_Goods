# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I track my order?
Give the support agent your order number (for example, ORD-00012) and it will
look up the carrier, tracking number, current status, and estimated delivery.

## How long do refunds take?
Once an eligible refund is processed it returns to your original payment method
within **5–7 business days**.

## Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
Yes — as long as the order hasn't shipped yet. Ask the agent with your order
number and the new address. After it ships, changes go through the carrier.

## Do you price match?
Northwind Goods does not offer price matching with other retailers at this time.

## Can I return a gift card?
No. Gift cards are final sale and cannot be returned or refunded.

## What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit and debit cards. Refunds always go back to the original
payment method used at checkout.
