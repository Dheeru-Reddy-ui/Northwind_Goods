# Return Policy

## Return window
Northwind Goods accepts returns within **30 days** of the delivery date. After
30 days we can no longer accept a return or issue a refund for a standard order.
Items must be in their original condition — unused, with tags and original
packaging where applicable.

## How to start a return
Tell our support agent your order number and the item you'd like to return, or
start a return from your account page. We'll email you a prepaid return label.
You don't pay return shipping for defective or incorrect items.

## Non-returnable items
Some items are final sale and cannot be returned or refunded at any time:
- Gift cards
- Final-sale and clearance items
- Perishable goods

## Condition and restocking
Returns arriving used, damaged by the customer, or missing parts may be refused
or subject to a partial refund. Northwind Goods does not charge a restocking fee
on standard returns within the window.

## Refund timing
Once a return is approved (or an eligible refund is processed), the amount is
returned to your original payment method within **5–7 business days**.
