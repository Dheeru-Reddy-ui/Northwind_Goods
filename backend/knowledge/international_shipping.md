# International Shipping

## Where we ship
Northwind Goods ships to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and the
EU. Availability of some items may vary by country due to local regulations.

## Delivery times and costs
International orders typically arrive in **7–14 business days** depending on
destination and customs. International shipping is calculated at checkout and is
**not** covered by the domestic free-shipping threshold.

## Duties and taxes
Import duties and taxes, where they apply, are the responsibility of the
recipient and are collected by the carrier or customs on delivery. Returns from
outside the US may take longer to process.
