# Contact & Support Hours

## Hours
Our support team (and this AI agent) are available:
- **Monday–Friday:** 8:00am – 8:00pm ET
- **Saturday:** 9:00am – 5:00pm ET
- **Sunday:** closed

The AI support agent can help around the clock for order status, refunds within
policy, address changes, and cancellations. Requests that need a human
specialist are queued and handled during the hours above.

- Chat with the support agent on our site
