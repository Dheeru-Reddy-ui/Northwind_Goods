# Damaged or Defective Items

## Reporting damage
If your item arrives damaged or defective, contact support **within 7 days** of
delivery with your order number. A photo of the damage helps us resolve it
faster but isn't required to start a claim.

## What we do
For a confirmed damaged or defective item we'll either:
- Send a **free replacement**, or
- Issue a **full refund**, including any shipping you paid.
You won't pay return shipping on a damaged item — we cover it with a prepaid label.

## Damaged in transit
If the package looks damaged on arrival, note it with the carrier if possible and
let us know. We handle the carrier claim on your behalf so you don't have to.
