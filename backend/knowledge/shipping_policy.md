# Shipping Policy

## Shipping methods and SLAs
- **Standard shipping** arrives in about **5 business days**.
- **Express shipping** arrives in about **2 business days**.
Business days exclude weekends and public holidays. You'll get a tracking number
by email once your order ships.

## Free shipping
Orders totaling **$50 or more** qualify for **free standard shipping**
automatically at checkout. Orders under $50 pay a flat standard shipping fee.

## Order processing
Most orders are processed and handed to the carrier within 1 business day. Until
an order ships you can still change the shipping address or cancel it; once it
has shipped, those changes must go through the carrier.

## Tracking and delays
If tracking shows your order as **delayed**, it usually catches up within a day
or two. If a shipment hasn't moved for several days, contact support and we'll
open a case with the carrier.
